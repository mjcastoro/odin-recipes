Chicken Parm

Recipe Description

Chicken parmesean is a delcious combination of fried chicken tenders with marinara sauce, mozzerella, and parmesean cheese on top

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oil
  • Mozzerella and Parmesean cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Filet the chicken breast into thin slices
  2. Crack egg into bowl with water and dip chicken breast in egg
  3. Coat chicken breast in breadcrumbs
  4. Fry the chicken in a pan with oil
  5. Put marinara sauce and cheese on top of chicken
  6. Cook in the oven at 400 degrees until cheese is melted
